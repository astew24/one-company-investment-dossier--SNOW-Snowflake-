## Investment Thesis

Snowflake is undervalued because its expansion into AI-driven data applications will accelerate product adoption and revenue growth over the next 3–5 years, while the market continues to price it primarily as a slowing data warehouse provider.

The market is anchoring on decelerating revenue growth and compressing margins without fully accounting for:
- Snowflake's structural advantage as the neutral data cloud (not tied to AWS/GCP/Azure ecosystems)
- Cortex AI and the emerging AI data application layer built on top of its platform
- Net Revenue Retention dynamics as enterprise cohorts mature and deepen usage
- The compounding effect of its Data Marketplace and partner ecosystem
