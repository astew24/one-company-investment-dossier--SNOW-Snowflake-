Investment Thesis

Snowflake is undervalued because its expansion into AI-driven data applications will accelerate product adoption and revenue growth over the next 3–5 years, while the market continues to price it primarily as a slowing data warehouse provider.

Key Angles
  • 1 Neutral data cloud not locked into any hyperscaler ecosystem — AWS, GCP, or Azure
  • 2 Cortex AI as an emerging application layer driving net-new monetization
  • 3 Net Revenue Retention compounding as enterprise cohorts deepen usage over time
  • 4 Data Marketplace and partner flywheel building durable switching costs
Key Risks
  • Customer concentration — large enterprise cohorts driving disproportionate revenue
  • AI commoditization could erode pricing power on core warehouse workloads
  • Hyperscaler pricing pressure from AWS Redshift, BigQuery, and Azure Synapse
  • Consumption-based model introduces revenue volatility in macro downturns
